---
title: "pstree"
---

Display a tree of processes in Linux.

`-p` to show PIDs, `-s` to show parents. E.g. `pstree -ps 4120`
`-h` to highlight stuff.

### Useful
You can view processes in [[KDE]] by pressing `Ctrl + Esc`.
